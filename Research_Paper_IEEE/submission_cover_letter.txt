Dear Editor,

I am pleased to submit the manuscript titled "PeopleFlow: Floor-Plan-Aware Simulation for Building Evacuation Safety Assessment" for consideration in IEEE Access.

This manuscript presents a reproducible simulation workflow for evacuation-safety assessment from architectural floor plans, with journal-scale evidence across a 450-run core matrix and supplementary validation and sensitivity studies. The work emphasizes comparative decision support for safety engineering and digital twin-enabled planning.

For reviewer visibility, we include a short supplementary multimedia file (peopleflow_multimedia_supplement.mp4) that demonstrates the end-to-end simulation workflow and scenario-comparison outputs.

The manuscript is original, has not been published previously, and is not under consideration elsewhere. All authors have approved the submission.
